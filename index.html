About Me

I am a Regulatory Affairs & Public Health Specialist with over 15 years of international experience in reliance-based registration pathways, quality management systems, and clinical operations. Currently working as a consultant at the World Health Organization (WHO), I coordinate multi-stakeholder projects, deliver cross-regional training, and provide technical regulatory advice to strengthen access to medicines and vaccines worldwide.

I am multilingual (Portuguese, English, French, Spanish), skilled in digital regulatory platforms (ePQS, Salesforce, DECISOR), and passionate about equity in access to healthcare. My background spans molecular biology, pharmacy, and sustainability, with strong expertise in project management, teaching, and global health systems.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week guided course by GRAPH Courses that equips professionals with practical AI skills for automating tasks, conducting research, and enhancing scientific communication. The program emphasizes no-code AI tools and hands-on applications for productivity in academia and global health.

Key Modules:

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations with AI
  • AI-Automated Data Analysis
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis
  • Custom GPTs and Chatbots
  • AI for Task Automation
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach
  • Capstone Project & Showcase
